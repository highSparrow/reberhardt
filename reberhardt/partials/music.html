Improvisation: Yolo Time

Most of the songs in "Yolo Time" were completely made up on the spot with no planning/practice; one person would start playing a riff, and then others would join in. Lyrics were improvised as well. This was the first time improvising for some of us, and we had fun playing musical chairs (rotating instruments), so sometimes we didn't even really know how to play the instrument, but we had lots of fun!
